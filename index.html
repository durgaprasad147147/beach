About Mypadu Beach

Mypadu Beach is a scenic beach located on the eastern coast, known for its golden sands and peaceful ambiance. It's a perfect spot for relaxation, family outings, and water sports enthusiasts. Discover local delicacies, enjoy the sunset, or simply unwind by the shore.
